About the business

A self-run web service built for small businesses that want a real local presence.

Find-Your-Site is based on a simple belief: small businesses should not need a giant agency to have a clean, trustworthy, and useful website.

Friendly, practical, personal

Websites should feel like an introduction, not a technical chore.

I help local companies turn their services, story, and personality into organized websites that customers can actually use. The focus is not just looking nice; it is making the next step obvious.

That could mean calling for a quote, viewing work examples, submitting an inquiry, reading reviews, or understanding what makes the business trustworthy.

Find-Your-Site values

Local-first: built around neighborhood trust and real customer needs.

Clear communication: simple pages, direct wording, and no confusing fluff.

Long-term care: websites should be updated, maintained, and improved over time.

Growth-minded: lead support is available for clients who want help finding opportunity.
