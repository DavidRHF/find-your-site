How to read this gallery

These mock sites are proof of structure, not just pretty pages.

Each example uses a different voice, industry, layout, and customer path. That helps you see how your site can be shaped around your own business instead of forced into one default style.

What you should notice

  • Clear home page message
  • Service or product pages
  • Personality-driven copy
  • Contact paths that are easy to find
  • Interactive features when they make sense for your business
